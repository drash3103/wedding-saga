SERVICES

Wedding Planning entails a wide range of services and it can be quite tedious for couples to identify credible vendors for each service. As leading wedding planners and event organizers, our large network of reliable professionals helps us bring the best to our clients for their wedding celebrations. Our team of wedding professionals assist you from beginning to end to ensure all your demands and wishes are fulfilled so that you can host one of the finest celebrations for your wedding day that your guests will cherish for a long time to come. Below are the range of services we offer.

Destination Management

As a renowned destination wedding planning company, we have successfully delivered some of the finest wedding experiences to our clients and their guests.From arranging the venues at any given destination across the countries, to managing the hotels, identifying the right venues for the events, to guest entertainment – we take care of everything.

Photography & videography

Revisiting and reliving epic moments along with close family and friends requires some of the most iconic photographs and video footage form the days of the events. We entrust reliable photo and video teams using the latest technology to capture and convert your moments to memories.

Guest Accommodatiom

Providing your guests with all the comfort and convenience is an essential part of your wedding celebrations. Our guest relations team assists you in identifying the ideal accommodation options across multiple room categories keeping your budgets and preferences in mind. Furthermore, we assist you in negotiating best pricing and including value added services which is a part of our wedding planning services.

menu planning & tasting

There is no denying that a carefully crafted wedding menu can be key to a successful wedding. Our culinary experts suggest and guide you through menu planning based on tastes, dietary requirements, and themes. A carefully curated menu heightens the guest experience at your events.

Wedding invitations

First impressions create lasting memories and hence your wedding invites should be unique and impressive. In the modern electronic age, destination weddings or intimate weddings have known to make use of the electronic medium to send out wedding invites.

Bachelor & Bachelorette Parties

So the boys want to meet and party before the groom embarks on a new phase of life. We know it best when it comes to men and their games. Also,Why should men have all the fun? Are you that bride who wants to have a hen’s night out with your girls before you tie the knot? We conceptualize themed bachelorette parties whether you and your friends can re-live your best memories and share a good laugh.

bridal care

Ensuring the bride is at her best on her big day is what our team of experts constantly strive towards. Skin Care, Hair Stylists, Ensemble Consultants, Henna / Mehendi Artists and so much more goes into preparing the bride for the most important celebration of her life.As your preferred destination wedding planners, we bring you the best wedding vendors in each area of Bridal Care to ensure that you enjoy the perfect desired look for your events.

vip management

Our VIP Management Unit follows all security protocols and provides the best protection for your high profile guests and dignitaries. Experienced and skilled officers ensure that your esteemed guests are provided with a stress-free experience to enjoy your wedding celebrations thoroughly

Travels & Logistics

Guest Arrivals experience, greeting at the airports, transfers to the venue are all a part of the guests’ first touchpoint at your destination weddings.Our professional Logistics Coordinators chalk out an effective wedding logistics and transportation plan for all your guests including airport transfers and city excursions while relieving you of any stress.
